Shravya Shenoy

Astronomy PhD Student @ University of Hertfordshire

Hi and welcome to my webpage! I am a PhD student at the Centre for Astrophysics Research (CAR)(iREx), based at University of Hertfordshire. My research focuses on understanding the evolution of local galaxies with LOFAR radio observations. In this context, I use a combination of machine learning algorithms and observational data to handle large datasets. .

Before moving to the UK, I completed my MSc in Astronomy Research at the University of Hertfordshire.

Please do not hesitate to ask questions about my work or reach out if you'd like to collaborate!
